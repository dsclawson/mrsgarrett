## Privacy Policy for Mrs. Garrett 

  Mrs. Garrett stores your mindmap data locally in your browser and optionally
  syncs to your Google Drive when you sign in. 
                                                                                                                                                                                        
  Data Collection:
  
  - All mindmap data stays in your browser's local storage                                                                                                                              
  - When you enable Google Drive sync, files are stored in your personal
    Google Drive in a folder you control 
  - We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers
  - Google Drive access is limited to files created by this extension

  Third Party Services:
  
  - Google Drive API (when you enable sync)

  You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings.
