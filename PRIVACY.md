# Privacy Policy for Mrs. Garrett

**Last Updated: November 24, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Mrs. Garrett ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our productivity application.

## Information Collection and Use

Mrs. Garrett is designed with privacy as a priority. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

### Information We Collect

Mrs. Garrett does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to external servers. All data remains local to your device.

### Data Storage

- All application data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No personal information is collected or shared with third parties

## Third-Party Services

Mrs. Garrett does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect or process your data.

## Cookies and Tracking

Mrs. Garrett does not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking mechanisms.

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your local device, the security of your information depends on the security measures you have implemented on your device. We recommend:

- Keeping your device's operating system up to date
- Using strong passwords or device encryption
- Following general security best practices for your device

## Children's Privacy

Mrs. Garrett does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is not directed at children, and we do not intentionally gather personal information from users under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights

Since Mrs. Garrett does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All data remains under your control on your local device.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository: https://github.com/dsclawson/mrsgarrett

## Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy regulations including:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies

## Summary

In summary, Mrs. Garrett:
- Does not collect personal information
- Does not transmit data to external servers
- Stores all data locally on your device
- Does not use tracking or analytics
- Does not share data with third parties
- Does not use cookies
